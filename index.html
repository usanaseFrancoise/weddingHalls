SOPHISTICATED COMFORT IN Kigali

The beautifully renovated Hotel Fullerton Anaheim is a sophisticated hotel located at the edge of Anaheim, California. With Disneyland® Resort, Downtown Disney®, the Anaheim Convention Center and Cal State Fullerton all just moments away and easily reached via our complimentary shuttle this distinctive hotel is the perfect choice for vacationing families, weekend getaways and executives doing business in the Town and Kigali areas
Feel completely at home in stylish and contemporary yet comfortable spaces for working, relaxing, dining, and gathering. Our newly refreshed hotel accommodations in Fullerton, CA rise to any occasion, work or play, with elegant décor, crystal clear high-definition televisions and plush beds draped with high thread count linens. Ultra luxurious Executive Guest Rooms and the lavish Presidential Suite are ideal for families, groups and business guests seeking additional living space and upgraded amenities.

In addition to our free local shuttle, thoughtful complimentary perks include high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity, parking and a 24-hour business center. Grab an energizing workout at our well-equipped fitness center or soak up the warm California sun by our heated, outdoor swimming pool and jacuzzi. Savor creative cuisine and unwind over cocktails at the Citizen Kitchen, a chic, sophisticated sports bar unlike any in Anaheim. For weddings and business functions, the hotel’s 15,000 square feet of event space includes three ballrooms, six smaller event venues and a beautifully landscaped garden courtyard. Each event we host is custom tailored, with personalized event planning and catering services. Reserve a room at The Hotel Fullerton Anaheim and discover just how affordable enjoying the best of Anaheim can be.
